Cost Optimization

Reduce cloud spend while improving reliability.

Cost optimization is not just about cutting spend. It is about building efficient systems that scale responsibly.

Optimization levers

  • Right size compute and storage resources.
  • Use autoscaling for variable workloads.
  • Adopt reserved capacity for stable workloads.

Governance model

  • Tagging policies for cost attribution.
  • Budget alerts and anomaly detection.
  • Monthly cost review with product owners.

Storage optimization

Lifecycle policies, tiered storage, and archival strategies.

Network efficiency

Reduce egress costs with caching and edge delivery.

Observability

Measure cost per request, per customer, and per product line.

Cost optimization plan

  • Inventory: map services and monthly spend.
  • Quick wins: remove idle resources and fix overprovisioning.
  • Long term: architecture updates for sustainable efficiency.
